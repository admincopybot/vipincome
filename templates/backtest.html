How Backtesting Works

Backtesting allows you to see what Income Machine would have recommended on a specific date in the past. This helps you understand the effectiveness of the scoring system over time.

The 5-Factor Technical Score System

Each ETF is scored based on these 5 technical factors:

Factor Description Points
Trend 1 Price is above the 20-day EMA 1
Trend 2 Price is above the 100-day EMA 1
Snapback RSI is under 50 1
Momentum Price is above previous week's close 1
Stabilizing 3-day ATR is less than 6-day ATR 1

ETFs with higher scores (4-5) are typically better candidates for income strategies. Backtesting helps you verify how these signals performed in the past.
