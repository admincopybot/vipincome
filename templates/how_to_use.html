How To Use Income Machine

Income Machine helps you find the highest-probability ETF options trades for consistent income. Follow these 4 simple steps to get your trade recommendations.

1

ETF Scoreboard

The ETF Scoreboard analyzes all major sector ETFs using our 5-factor technical analysis system. Each ETF receives a score from 1-5, indicating its technical strength for option income strategies.

How to use this step: Review the ETF scores and select an ETF with a score of 4 or 5 for the best results. Click the "Select" button next to any ETF to continue to the Asset Review step.

2

Asset Review

The Asset Review page provides a detailed breakdown of the 5 technical factors for your selected ETF. This helps you understand why the ETF received its score and what technical signals it's currently showing.

How to use this step: Review the technical indicators and market analysis. If you're satisfied with the ETF's technical profile, click "Choose This ETF" to proceed to the Strategy Selection step.

3

Strategy Selection

The Strategy Selection page allows you to choose between three income approaches based on your personal risk tolerance and return objectives.

Options available:

  • Aggressive Income: 7-15 days DTE, ~30% ROI, higher risk
  • Steady Income: 14-30 days DTE, ~22% ROI, balanced risk/reward
  • Passive Income: 30-45 days DTE, ~16% ROI, lower risk

How to use this step: Select the strategy that aligns with your income goals and risk tolerance. Click on any strategy card to proceed to the final step.

4

Trade Details

The final step displays specific option spread opportunities for your selected ETF and strategy. Each trade shows the key metrics including expiration, strike price, credit, ROI, and probability of profit.

How to use this step: Review the available spread opportunities and select the one that best fits your account size and preferences. You can then place this trade with your broker.

Frequently Asked Questions

An ETF option spread is an options strategy that involves buying and selling options on Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) at different strike prices. This approach limits risk while allowing for consistent income generation. Income Machine focuses primarily on credit spreads, where you receive a premium upfront when opening the position.

ETF scores are calculated using our proprietary 5-factor technical analysis system. The factors include:
  1. Short-Term Trend: Price position relative to 20-day EMA
  2. Long-Term Trend: Price position relative to 50-day EMA
  3. Snapback Potential: RSI momentum signals
  4. Weekly Momentum: Weekly close price dynamics
  5. Volatility Stabilization: ATR patterns and trends
Each factor contributes one point to the final score, ranging from 1-5.

The strategy you choose should align with your personal risk tolerance and income goals:
  • Aggressive Income: Best for experienced traders with higher risk tolerance who prioritize maximizing returns.
  • Steady Income: Suitable for most traders seeking a balance between consistent returns and manageable risk.
  • Passive Income: Ideal for conservative traders who prioritize capital preservation and more predictable, though lower, returns.
If you're new to options trading, we recommend starting with the Passive or Steady Income strategies.

ETF scores are updated daily based on market conditions. We recommend checking Income Machine at least once per week to identify new opportunities. If you're using the Aggressive strategy with shorter timeframes (7-15 days), you may want to check more frequently as new trades will be needed more often.
